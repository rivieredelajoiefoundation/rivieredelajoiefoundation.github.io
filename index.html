About Riviere de la Joie

Riviere de la joie is a nonprofit organization whose focus includes vulnerable people, particularly young people, women, widows, and those with disabilities and all those who are living in situations of risk in rural and remote areas and peri urban settings.

RIVIERE DE LA JOIE cultivates...the culture of self-reliance and self-belonging through empowerment, and with overall purpose of instilling into people the culture of love, kindness and compassion that seem to fade into our societies, The Foundation provides support on Sexual and Reproductive Health Rights, livelihood/food security, sports/talent promotions, Peace and security, Quality Education and Mentorship for young people enabling them to live their full potentials.

Our Recent activities

Our Mission

To empower and promote sustainable development projects involving vulnerable in the community through economic empowerment and civic education on livelihood and food security, sports and talent promotion, SRHR, Peace and security hence enhancing their quality of life.

Our Vission:

We aspire to develop a holistic individual by dissolving the discriminatory societal barriers by promoting acceptability, love, kindness and compassion, empowerment and contentment of young people, women, those with disabilities and those living in situation of risks.

What we do

We Empower & provide a platform for the vulnerable people to realize their hidden potentials through and by employing key strategies: Mentorship, Vocational Trainings , Economic empowerment, Community engagement and human rights activities, Community Outreach Programs.
