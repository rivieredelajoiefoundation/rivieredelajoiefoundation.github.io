Our project List

HERA KURIBIKE WIGIRE

This project consissts of identifying small vendors, mainly made of women, widows, teen mothers from poor families and all other people that are financially challenged, we reach out to them, mobilize them to form tontines(saving groups), we pay for their community based health insurance and their family members. Then, we train them about entreprenuership, and give them small capital to carry out their small commercial acivities of their choice and more importantly we mentor them all along the journey.

INZOZI ZABA IMPAMO

With this Project, we are training Bicycle Taxmen in Bugarama , and its environs ... about Traffic Laws , as a way to pave their ways to achieve their dreams of becoming Motorcycle Taximen or even Drivers of vehicles , Trucks , buses , Lorries and so forth ..... In addition to create awareness about Traffic Laws , to minimize road accidents , which in most of the cases , it's claimed lives of many, and the records show , the main of those accidents is mainly the lack of the knowledge of Traffic Laws , and how to use road adequately

FIGHTING TEENAGE PREGNANCIES AND REHABILITATION OF TEEN MOTHERS

In this project we carry out numerous activities responding to 3 dimensions of interventions: 1. Preventing Phase for the Preventing phase, we do activities aiming at inhibiting causes of teenage pregnancies through sensitizations of the mass, traing of the youths, children structures and parents sensitizing to own the battle against this scourge. We at Riviere de la joie Foundation call it, "Dangerous animal we ought to attack together as one." we use the media both classic and social media platforms to amplify the voice and make it heard by the masses 2. Responding Phase we do train the victims and do the Mentorship program to mentor them, and help revive their hopes that due to pregnancy, one gets discouraged and have her hope for bright future fade, it's against that backdrop, we do Mentorship to restore their hope and help them catchup with their visions and dreams. 3. Reporting Phase We do the alert system, named listing points and YOU SEE SOMETHING YOU SAY SOMETHING approach tailored to gather information about teenage pregancies and suspicious behaviour that can lead to teenage pregnancy from a family to the community reach. we collaborate with the government structures that have children's right into their mandate Police, RIB, at sector level.

Activities for Fighting Teenage Pregnancies and Rehabilitation of Teen mothers

Establishing a pilot listening point system in one district where teenagers can come/call to seek support and guidance. Conduct a Training of Trainers (ToT) for members of the former National Children Council, and National Youth Council at the sector level on Sexual and Reproductive Health (SRH) Conduct monthly radio talk shows on community radios on issues affecting teenagers such as teenage pregnancies Conduct a sensitization campaign into secondary schools targeting teenage boys on abstinence till marriage and change of sexual behavior at school and in their communities. Set up "ASK THE EXPERT" platform where teens can reach out to seek advice on Sexual and Reproductive using e-mail or social media. Whereby A weekly recap will be published on our Youtube Channel to reach more teenagers. Rehabilitate teen mothers by following them up in their communities, teaching them a skill that can help them to live a dignified life. Conduct a sensitization campaign to encourage parents to have conversations with their children on Sexual and Reproductive Health and creating a bond where parents are the first point of reference when children need advice

CHARITY AS RESPONSIBILITY NOT A FAVOR

Charity as a RESPONSIBILITY not a Favor , is one of our Main Projects , that spearhead our work, as our overall Objective is to Instill and or impart into the people the culture of kindness, generosity, compassion , love and care , as a sure way towards building an enabling environment for all, Whereby , the culture of sharing would be opted by everyone, in place of selfishness. Within this project, we will organize and surprise poor families , especially widows , orphans and those living in tough situation , identified randomly and help them , with food stuffs, home utensils , clothing , small capitals for small commerce ( businesses ) where it would be possible.

FIGHTING AGAINST DRUG ABUSE

Conduct monthly awareness campaigns on drug abuse in youth and teenagers in partnership with National Police and other community influencers such as sportsmen and artists Conduct monthly radio talk shows on community radios where recovered drug abusers can give testimonies and advice to the youth and teens on the bad effects of drug abuse. Conduct annual sports competitions as a platform to reach out to more youth and engage them on the effects of drug abuse. Set up "ASK THE EXPERT" platform where teens can reach out to seek advice using e-mail or social media. A weekly recap will be published in the newspaper to reach more teenagers. Work with schools and youth leadership to establish anti-drugs use clubs in school and communities
